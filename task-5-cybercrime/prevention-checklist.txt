Stay Safe Online

1. General Safety Tips

•	The sender of a message should also be checked by email before any message is relied upon.
•	Do not open unrecognized or suspicious links that come through e-mail or SMS.
•	Do not reveal your passwords, OTPs or any personal information to anyone.
•	Use strong and distinctive passwords to different accounts.
•	Turn on a two factor Authentication (2FA) on all significant accounts.
•	Do not use open Wi-Fi to log into important accounts.
•	Only download apps and other software on official websites or app stores.
•	Change your passwords and security requirements regularly.

2. Financial / UPI Safety Tips

•	Never grant payment requests that are not known or collect requests on UPI apps.
•	One should never send money without verifying UPI ID or the QR code.
•	Should you not disclose your UPI PIN to anybody in any circumstance.

3. Reporting Cybercrime

•	Report on: National Cyber Crime Portal - cybercrime.gov.in.
•	Helpline Number: 1930 (24x7 support)
