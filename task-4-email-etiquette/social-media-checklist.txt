Social Media Checklist

DO's :
•	Have a professional and clear profile picture.
•	Cogitate before publishing anything on the internet.
•	Ensure that your account privacy settings are kept updated
•	Post helpful, good, and valuable content.
•	Be respectful to other people and speak in a polite manner.

DON'Ts :
•	Do not publish personal and sensitive data.
•	Do not publish offensive and inappropriate material.
•	Do not fake news or unconfirmed news.
•	Do not argue and post hateful comments online.
•	Do not use other people to abuse their photographs or work.
